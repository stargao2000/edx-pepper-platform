WHO WE ARE


PCG Education is a division of Public Consulting Group (PCG), a leading provider of management consulting and technology to help public sector education, health, human services, and other government clients achieve their performance goals and better serve populations in need. PCG Education currently works with more than 4,000 school districts and has contracts with 13 state education agencies to provide services and software solutions that focus on student achievement. More information about PCG is available at Publicconsultinggroup.com.




WestEd, a national nonpartisan, nonprofit research, development, and service agency, works with education and other communities to promote excellence, achieve equity, and improve learning for children, youth, and adults. WestEd has 16 offices nationwide, from Washington and Boston to Arizona and California. Its corporate headquarters are in San Francisco. More information about WestEd is available at WestEd.org.




Since 1995, Edu2000 has been the primary developer of visual online content for several state departments of education, school districts, and educational publishers. Edu2000 is particularly strong in the development of online mathematics and science curriculum that includes interactive animations, simulations, experiments, and "visual dictionary" products that teach concepts to all learners. Edu2000 is also experienced in custom development for interactive digital content, technology enhanced assessment items, and online professional development materials. More information about Edu2000 is available at www.education2000.com.
